What is hosting?

After the development of your website is done, you need to "publish" it on the web to allow internet users to find and access your website. This process includes extensive research to understand the following:

  • Deciding on which hosting service do you need among: Shared-hosting, VPS, Dedicated Server, and cloud hosting
  • Choosing one of many hosting service providers that has the best cost to value ratio (like uptime, performance, customer service, etc...) such as : hostgator, blueHost, dreamHost, Hostinger and many many others
  • Picking the most suitable package for your needs from the many choices given by the hosting service provider that you decided on
  • Buying a domain from the best registrar and procteting your domain with domain privacy which prevents sharing your personal info online for spam.
  • Maintaining an SSL Certificate for your website which offers protection against hackers.
  • All these services are being renewed monthly or annually, so there's the burden of always ensuring that you are using your money to the fullest or is it time to get a better host/package.
